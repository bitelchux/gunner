Gunner

How to play

Fire your gun by clicking/tapping on the spot you wish to fire at. Hold down click/tap for continuous fire.

You have 3 types of shot, each consuming different amounts of energy:

  • Small (regular) shot - fast firing, good for spraying weak enemies. Consumes 1 energy per shot. (Keyboard shortcut: 1)
  • Large shot - Slower, but better against tougher enemies. Consumes 3 energy per shot. (Keyboard shortcut: 2)
  • Burst shot - Slow strong round that bursts into 5 small shots after dealing damage. Consumes 8 energy per shot. (Keyboard shortcut: 3)

You regain energy by destroying enemies. Tougher enemies give more energy, but still counts as 1 kill.

When your energy reaches 0, it's game over.
